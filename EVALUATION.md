## Challenge outcome loop

Resolve 90 implements an operational behavior loop:

1. **Understand** normalized incident evidence.
2. **Track** freshness, pressure, accessible capacity, transit fit, and ownership.
3. **Act** through a sequenced role-specific response.
4. **Predict** modeled counterfactual impact.
5. **Verify** evidence references and deterministic guardrails.
6. **Optimize** only among safety- and accessibility-eligible plans.
7. **Scale** through venue-agnostic ports and role relays.
8. **Learn** through an immutable audit-port receipt and documented scaling path.
